Timeline of My Life

by Jhane Rose Sadicon

Humble Beginnings

As the eldest daughter, I felt the weight of responsibility to set an example—expected to be the role model and the dependable achiever. From a young age, I was consistently recognized for my academic performance, balancing family expectations with a growing curiosity for learning and excellence.

Television Feature

I was featured on ABS-CBN News TV Patrol and GMA’s 24 Oras during the RoboSports Tournament 2023 held at De La Salle Medical and Health Sciences Institute, showcasing my early involvement in robotics.

Women's Month Recognition

I was featured in JuanaKnow, a Women’s Month campaign that honored my contributions in robotics and technology. It was a proud moment to be celebrated as a young woman breaking barriers and excelling in the STEM field, as I fully embraced my role as a trailblazer.

STEMAZING and Robotics Awards

The journey continued as I triumphed at the 2023 Division Science and Technology Fair, Robotics Competition cum STEMAZING event. I received multiple awards that reflected my dedication and consistent performance in science and technology.

Microsoft Youth Ambassador

I proudly graduated as a Microsoft Youth Ambassador 2.0, earning the 3rd Best Capstone Award in recognition of my commitment and contributions to empowering learners and advancing 21st-century skills. Serving as the Technical Head of Region IV-A CALABARZON at Rizal High School in Pasig.

National Robotics Quest Champion

A thrilling triumph unfolded at the 20th National Science Quest Robotics Technology event held at Skyrise Hotel, Baguio. I proudly secured 1st place in three categories: 500g Sumobot, 1kg Sumobot, and Line Tracing.

Best Research Paper Award

As one of the authors of a team research innovation, I was proud to see our work on sustainable concrete alternatives earn the prestigious Best Research Paper Award at the 4th Student Research Conference held at De La Salle University Dasmariñas - SHS. It was an exhilarating experience that reaffirmed my commitment to creating a better and more sustainable future.

Senior High Graduation

I graduated with honors from the STEM strand, completing senior high school with flying colors. My accomplishments in both academics and co-curricular activities were celebrated, symbolizing the result of years of dedication, perseverance, and passion. As Taylor Swift once said in her graduation speech, “We are all a patchwork quilt of those who have loved us, those who have believed in our futures”—and in that moment, I carried every lesson, every challenge, and every cheer into the next chapter of my journey.

A Meaningful Connection

Throughout my academic and tech-driven journey, I had the opportunity to meet Leinad Clark Dela Cruz, who, unbeknownst to me, had been admiring me since Grade 3. Over time, he went from being a supportive friend to becoming my favorite person. With Leinad, I learned how important it is to be emotionally open and how valuable it is to embrace new experiences and adventures. This helped me step outside my usual focus on robotics and research. Our relationship has encouraged me to see things from different perspectives and try new things.

Senior Robotics Athlete

The excitement reached new heights at the Summer Robot Games International Open, where I earned 3rd Runner-Up in the IBEAM Line Tracing – Senior Category. It was another thrilling opportunity to showcase my skills and passion for competitive robotics.

Perpetualite in Progress

Now that I’m pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a Specialization in Game Development, I feel like I’m exactly where I’m meant to be. I hope to inspire others to follow their interests in technology, and I’m excited to see where this journey takes me next.
